# kochi-tdr-model
# Kochi TDR Policy Sandbox

This repository presents a simulation-based exploration of Transfer of Development Rights (TDR) as a policy instrument for managing urban growth in Kochi, Kerala.

The work combines:
- A NetLogo agent-based model (ABM)
- A 27-run BehaviorSpace policy experiment
- An interactive Streamlit dashboard

Together, these allow exploration of how development, conservation, and land value interact under different policy settings.

## What the Model Does

The model simulates how:

- Developers respond to land value, accessibility, and policy incentives  
- Ecologically sensitive and heritage areas act as *sending zones*  
- Transit corridors and commercial nodes act as *receiving zones*  
- TDR enables transfer of development potential across space  

Development, conservation, and transfers evolve dynamically over time.

## Running the Model (Browser Version)

Open the browser model and click:

1. **Setup**
2. **GO**

The simulation will run for ~200 ticks, showing:

- Development expansion  
- TDR transfers  
- Conservation outcomes
